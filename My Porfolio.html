01 Affordable Housing Project

This project aimed at solving urban decay in the Bahati, Makadara region of Nairobi. The primary objective of this project was to provide safe, affordable, and sustainable housing options for middle to low-income residents within a self-sufficient neighborhood. This 20 minute neighborhood promotes sustainable, healthy, and inclusive urban living which is essential in planning our cities and urban areas.

02 Tigoni Villas

This 4 bedroom villa was designed as a centerpiece collection for an upcoming estate in the suburbs of Kiambu. Multiple design options were explored resulting in a timeless contemporary blend of clean lines, glass elements, and natural materials. The villa boasts of spacious rooms and large windows which flood the interior with natural light and offer stunning views of the lush surroundings.

03 Shell Hyrax Nakuru

I was involved in creating visualizations for various Shell Stations for Vivo Energy, in collaboration with KFC Africa and Simbisa Brands. The station emphasizes a clean and welcoming environment, with well-designed layout plans that optimize traffic flow and ensure ease of use. They offer a comprehensive range of services, including fueling stations, car wash facilities, and the respective restaurants, all designed with a strong focus on customer convenience and comfort.
